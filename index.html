Shivendra Singh Verma

I am a final year undergraduate student of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I am interested in guidance and control of vehciles particulary in field of marine apllications. Currently my work is on controller development for Autnomous Surface Vehicles(ASVs) under the guidance of Prof. Abhilash Somayajula. I have worked on implemention of Target-Attacker-Defender games on ROS under the guidance of Prof. Vishwanadha Reddy and Prof. Bharath Bhikkaji. Currently I am learning Reinforcement Learning. I am the head of the student Mars rover team of IIT Madras, Team Anveshak, which takes part in University Rover Challenge, held at Mars Desert Research Station, Utah. I have worked in the team as Embedded Systems and Control Engineer.
